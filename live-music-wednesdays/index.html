Live Music Wednesdays!

Tommy Bradshaw

Come on out to Bradshaw's and experience live folk music Wednesday mornings.  Every Wednesday this summer we will have a local folk music group here at the restaurant sharing their craft with you as you enjoy our craft: Biscuits!

Here is the line up - you will not want to miss it:

  • Oak & Ivy Harmony - June 1
  • The Meadowlark Minstrels - June 8
  • The Riverside Ramblers - June 15
  • Crimson Clover Collective - June 22
  • TBD - June 29
  • Pinecone Poets - July 6
  • Harmony Hollow - July 13
  • TBD - July 20
  • The Wandering Whippoorwills - July 27
  • Willow Creek Quartet - Aug 3

Here is what people are saying about the experience:

Last week at Bradshaw's, The Riverside Ramblers took us on an unforgettable musical journey. Their blend of soulful folk melodies and foot-tapping rhythms perfectly complemented the homely atmosphere of our restaurant. Each song they performed was a masterpiece of storytelling, rich with emotion and the spirit of folk tradition. Their music, as comforting and heartwarming as our signature biscuits, left our customers enchanted and asking for more.

Krystal - Henderson, KY

 

Harmony Hollow's performance at Bradshaw's was nothing short of magical. Their unique fusion of traditional folk with modern undertones was the perfect soundtrack for an evening of good food and great company. Their charismatic presence and engaging melodies created a lively yet intimate atmosphere. Customers were not only treated to the best biscuits in town but also to a musical experience that touched their souls and lifted their spirits.

Edward - Evansville, IN

Be sure to join us Wednesday for music and the best biscuits north of the Ohio river!  Don't know where we are, have never had a Bradshaw's biscuit? - Visit us here today!
